    Gyudon #

    Ingredients #

    • 1 Onion
    • Thinly sliced beef (fattier is better)
    • Ginger
    • Konbu
    • Sugar
    • Sake
    • Optional: Egg
    • Soy sauce

    Prep #

    1. Slice onion
    2. Slice beef
    3. Slice ginger in thin strips
    4. Make Konbu dashi

    Cook #

    1. Oil in pan, low heat
    2. Add ginger (cook until translucent)
    3. Add onion (Medium heat)
    4. Add salt
    5. Add konbu dashi
    6. Add sugar 20g
    7. Boil 5min
    8. Add Beef
    9. Sake
    10. Remove scum
    11. Add soy sauce
    12. Cook/simmer 10-15 min
